About Me:

Süisse is a DJ/producer hailing from London, England. He has been performing in Doha for 4 years in a range of venues for a range of different audiences. He mixes a unique style of tech, electro and deep house creating a distinctive atmosphere where ever he plays. He has had the pleasure of supporting acts such as The Mambo Brothers, Ben Santiago and Lovely Laura in the past.
